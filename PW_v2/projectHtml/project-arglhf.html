March 7th 2023 [Personal Project / For Fun]

AR_GLHF

At the time, I was playing a lot of CookieRun: OvenBreak, an auto-runner game. I saw it as a great opportunity to learn the fundamentals of game development and get comfortable with the Unity engine. One of the biggest challenges was implementing a respawn system, as the environment was fully auto-generated. After some experimentation, I resolved it by creating a checker that followed the player to manage respawn points effectively.

Another challenge I encountered was the player character sticking to the sides of platforms due to the constant forward motion of the auto-runner. This resulted in awkward interactions when colliding from the side. To address this, I implemented a wall-jump mechanic, which not only solved the issue but also significantly improved the overall feel and fluidity of the movement.
